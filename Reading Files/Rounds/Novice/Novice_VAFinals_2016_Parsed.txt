 What case is used to express place to which, extent of space, and duration of time? ANSWER: ACCUSATIVE BONUS: What case is used in partitive expressions? ANSWER: GENITIVE
 Which king of Pontus, whom Rome defeated mildly at battles such as Chaeronea, Orchomenus, and Lycus, was finally defeated in 63 B.C.? ANSWER: MITHRIDATES VI/THE GREAT BONUS: Who rushed peace talks with Mithridates in 84 B.C. because he was in a hurry to get back to Rome and deal with political trouble there?	 ANSWER: (LUCIUS CORNELIUS) SULLA
 What was the title of the chief priest of Jupiter? ANSWER: FLĀMEN DIALIS BONUS: What was the term for a fortune-teller who used animal entrails to provide his prophesies?	 ANSWER: HARUSPEX
 Give the genitive singular forms of hic, ille, ego, and tū.  ANSWER: HUIUS, ILLIUS, MEĪ, TUĪ BONUS: Give the accusative singular form in all three genders for hic.  ANSWER: HUNC (M.), HANC (F.), HOC (N.) 
 Who were the two rivalrous sons of Septimius Severus who ruled jointly until one murdered the other? ANSWER: CARACALLA, GETA BONUS: Name Severus’ second wife who bore him these sons. ANSWER: JULIA DOMNA
 What hero slept a night upon the altar of Athena, dreaming that the goddess gave him a golden bridle and instructed him to sacrifice a bull to Poseidon the Tamer, and awoke to find the bridle on the ground beside him, which he then used to tame the winged Pegasus? ANSWER: BELLEROPHON BONUS: Who was the old seer who instructed him to sleep upon the altar? ANSWER: POLYIDUS
 Translate the Latin motto of the state of Kansas: ad astra per aspera?  ANSWER: TO THE STARS THROUGH DIFFICULTIES BONUS: What is the Latin motto of Oklahoma?	 ANSWER: LABOR OMNIA VINCIT
