 Which of the following was NOT a color belonging to a racing faction in Ancient Rome: red, silver, white, blue, green? ANSWER: SILVER  BONUS: What were the other two colors adopted by racing factions, later added during the reign of Domitian? ANSWER: PURPLE AND GOLD 
 Which of the following is NOT derived from the Latin verb faciō: fascinate, facilitate, faculty, feasible, fortify? ANSWER: FASCINATE  BONUS: Which of the following NOT derived from the verb teneō: continent, continual, contingent, container? ANSWER: CONTINGENT 
 Against what enemy did the tribune Sulpicius Rufus passed a law in 88 B.C., to grant command to Marius? ANSWER: MITHRIDATES  BONUS: To whom had the senate assigned this command? ANSWER: (L. CORNELIUS) SULLA (FELIX) 
 When he landed in Carthage, Aeneas had a one-year relationship with the Queen of the city. Who was this queen? ANSWER: DIDO/ELISSA  BONUS: However, Aeneas had a previous wife while back in Troy who died before he left. Who was this woman? ANSWER: CREUSA 
 When recognized, perform the following commands: Salī et dīc nōmen tuum. ANSWER: STUDENT SHOULD JUMP AND SAY HIS/HER NAME  BONUS: Now perform these commands: Tenēte manūs et cantāte carmen nātiōnale foederatārum civitātium . ANSWER: ALL TEAMMATES SHOULD GRAB HANDS AND SING THE NATIONAL ANTHEM.
